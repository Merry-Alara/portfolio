Who Am I?

I'm Maryam Anwar, a passionate individual who loves to share stories and create meaningful experiences. From exploring the beauty of nature to embracing the digital era, I aim to inspire and be inspired.

My Education

I began my academic journey in the Pre-Medical field, where I developed a strong foundation in analytical thinking and problem-solving. Later, I secured admission to Lahore Garrison University, where I am currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in Digital Forensics and Cybersecurity. During my studies, I have gained expertise in areas such as ethical hacking, malware analysis, and incident response, complemented by hands-on experience in digital forensic tools. I worked on significant projects, including forensic analysis of encrypted data and developing secure communication protocols. Additionally, I earned certifications such as Certified Computer and Forensic Analyst (CCFA) and Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH), enhancing my knowledge in cybersecurity practices. As part of my practical experience, I completed an internship at Digital Forensic and Cybersecurity Research Center, focusing on penetration testing and vulnerability assessments.
